## Reasearch on Data Governance

## Intoduction
Data governance is a framework of principles, policies, roles, and standards that ensures an organization's data is accurate, secure, compliant, and usable throughout its lifecycle.

It enhances PII by establishing a structured framework of policies, procedures, and technical controls that ensure data is identified, protected, and handled lawfully.
